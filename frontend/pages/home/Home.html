Revolutionizing Parking Management

Developed by passionate software engineering students from Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, our parking management system addresses the daily challenges of modern workplace parking.

Through innovative technology including real-time monitoring, QR-based permits, and intelligent violation detection, we're creating a seamless, paperless parking ecosystem that benefits everyone - from administrators to daily users.
