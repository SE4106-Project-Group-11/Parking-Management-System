About us

Welcome to the Vehicle Parking Management System – a smart, secure, and user-friendly solution developed to revolutionize how parking is handled in modern institutions and workplaces. Designed by a team of passionate software engineering undergraduates from the Faculty of Computing, Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, our system addresses the daily challenges of parking allocation, permit issuance, and violation enforcement. Through real-time space monitoring, QR-based permits, and streamlined digital access for employees, visitors, and non-employees, we aim to reduce congestion, boost operational efficiency, and enhance overall parking experiences. Our mission is to deliver a seamless, paperless, and transparent parking ecosystem that benefits both administrators and users alike.

Features We Provide

  • Employee registration and secure login system
  • Apply for digital parking permits with QR codes
  • Admin-assisted visitor and non-employee registration
  • Real-time parking space availability tracking
  • Parking violation notifications and fine enforcement
  • Online rent and fine payments for non-employees
  • Admin dashboard with live activity and reports
