Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our vehicle parking management services.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our parking management services.

Personal Information

  • • Full name and contact details
  • • Email address and phone number
  • • Account credentials and preferences
  • • Profile photo (optional)

Payment Information

  • • Credit/debit card details
  • • Billing address and history
  • • Payment preferences

Vehicle Information

  • • License plate number
  • • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • • Vehicle color and type
  • • Registration and insurance details

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve our parking management services.

Service Provision

  • • Process parking reservations and payments
  • • Manage parking space availability
  • • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • • Provide customer support services

Security & Compliance

  • • Monitor parking violations and enforcement
  • • Prevent fraudulent activities
  • • Comply with local parking regulations
  • • Maintain security surveillance records

Service Improvement

  • • Analyze usage patterns and preferences
  • • Optimize parking space allocation
  • • Develop new features and services
  • • Conduct user experience research

Communication

  • • Send service updates and notifications
  • • Provide parking alerts and reminders
  • • Share promotional offers (with consent)
  • • Respond to inquiries and feedback

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in specific circumstances as outlined below.

We may share your information with:

  • • Law enforcement: When required by law or court order
  • • Parking authorities: For violation enforcement and compliance
  • • Service providers: Third-party vendors who assist our operations
  • • Business partners: Integrated parking facility operators
  • • Emergency services: In case of safety or security incidents
  • • Legal advisors: When necessary for legal proceedings

We do not sell your personal information

We never sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Any data sharing is strictly limited to the purposes outlined in this policy.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and misuse.

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Access Control

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data.

Monitoring

Continuous monitoring systems detect and prevent unauthorized access attempts.
